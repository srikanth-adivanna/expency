Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, but no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove your personal data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and do not have access to your expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries as promptly as possible.
